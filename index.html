Paul McKarris

Summary

I am currently an undergraduate student in mathematical physics at the University of Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. My main hobbies are basketball, philosophy, physics and mathematics. In philosophy, I am mostly interested in the philosophy of language, metaphysics, and the philosophy of physics. In physics, my main interests are quantum gravity and quantum foundations. In mathematics, I am interested in logic and mathematical physics.
