Spagetti alla carbonara

  • 350g of spaghetti
  • 40g + as much as you want of pecorino romano
  • 20 g parmesean
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 120g guanciale
  • salt, pepper
Steps:
  1. Start boiling the water
  2. Cut the guanciale into rectangular parallelepiped of 0.5x0.5x3 cm^3. Start cooking at low heat
  3. Grate the pecorino and the parmesan and mix it with the egg yolks and 5cL or water. Mix until it becomes creamy. Salt and pepper.
  4. When the water is boiling, throw salt into it and boil the spaghetti. Brown the guanciale until they become crisp. Try to get the guanciale and the spaghetti ready together.
  5. Drain the pasta and keep a little cooking water. Put it all in a glass bowl and put the guanciale directly onto it. Mix well. Then pour the mixture of egg in the boil and stir so it hardens slighlty without getting scrambled.
  6. Serve in a plate and add pecorino and pepper.
