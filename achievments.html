My achievment and work experience

2019 CERN Summer Student Programme

In summer 2019 I got selected to participate at CERN Summer Student Programme where I attending a series of lectures ranging for accelerator and detector physics to quantum field theory. I was also assigned a research project consisting for commissioning a beta setup for time resolution measurements. I wrote a report that is uploaded on CERN Document Server.

2018 Canadian Astroparticle Physics Summer School

In summer 2018, I got selected to attend Canadian Astroparticle Physics Summer School hosted by the Arthur B. McDonald Canadian Astroparticle Physics Research Institute. Where I learned about the following topics:
  • Astrophysical evidence for dark matter, cosmology of dark matter, models and signals of dark matter, direction detection of dark mark matter
  • Neutrino oscillations; the SNO experiment, neutrino mass theory and experiment, neutrinoless double-beta decay
We also visited the 2015 Nobel Prize winning experiement SNO+ at SNOLAB and other experiments in SNOLAB.

2017 CERN Beamline for Schools Competition

In 2017, I gathered people from my highschool and we won CERN's Beamline for Schools competition. We designed an experiment to search for frationally charged particles. To participate we had to write proposal and filmed a one-minute video
. We got covered by many journals for our achievements, such as Toronto Star and CBC.
