2018 Canadian Astroparticle Physics Summer School

In summer 2018 I got accepted to the Canadian Astroparticle Physics Summer School hosted by the Arthur B. McDonald Canadian Astroparticle Physics Research Institute. During this we got lectures about:
  • Astrophysical evidence for dark matter, cosmology of dark matter, models and signals of dark matter, direction detection of dark mark matter
  • Neutrino oscillations; the SNO experiment, neutrino mass theory and experiment, neutrinoless double-beta decay
We also visited the 2015 Nobel Prize winning experiement SNO+ at SNOLAB and other experiments in SNOLAB.

2017 CERN Beamline for Schools Competition

In 2017, I gathered people from my highschool and participate at CERN's Beamline for Schools competition. We designed a experiment to serach for frationally charged particles. We wrote a proposal and filmed a video
We got covered by many journals for our achievements, such as, Toronto Star and CBC.
